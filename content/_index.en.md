## What is "Laconic"?

Referencing the ancient inhabitants of Laconia known for their more concise and terse style of speech, <em>Laconic</em> also describes a manner of speaking or writing that uses only a few words, often to express complex thoughts and ideas.

It's the perfect name for technology that simplifies the complexity of blockchain and accelerates DApp development.

## What sets Laconic apart?

The potential of blockchain technology is currently hampered by the middleware that connects it to actual applications. Some solutions already exist to index and store blockchain data, but they rely on centralized monopolist services to index and serve the data; and they accept and use data that cannot be cryptographically proven to be authentic and accurate.
